F.A.Q

How to reach the venue?

The hackathon will be held at Sri Ramakrishana Institute of Technology, Pachapalayam, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. For detailed directions, please see the footer map.

College Bus Route:
Pachapalayam pirivu ➔ SRIT campus
SRIT campus ➔ Pachapalayam pirivu

Will food be provided during the event?

Yes! Lunch will be provided to all participants to keep your energy levels high for the challenges ahead.

Is on-the-spot registration available?

All participants must register online for the Hackathon and Workshops on or before 12th April 2025, and for CodeIT on or before 15th April 2025.

On-spot registration is available ONLY for technical, non-technical, and cultural events. Please bring your college ID.

Is accommodation available?

Yes, accommodation is available at a charge of ₹250 per day. This includes both dinner and breakfast facilities.
