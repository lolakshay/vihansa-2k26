      F.A.Q

      • How to reach the venue?

        The hackathon will be held at Sri Ramakrishana Institute of Technology, Pachapalayam,Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. For detailed directions see footer map.

        College bus will be provided from Pachapalayam pirivu bus stop:
        Pachapalayam pirivu - SRIT campus
        SRIT campus - Pachapalayam pirivu

      • Will food be provided during the event?

        Yes! Lunch will be provided to all participants.

      • Is on-the-spot registration available?

        All participants must register online for the Hackathon and Workshops on or before 12th April 2025, and for CodeIT on or before 15th April 2025. On-spot registration will be available only for technical, non-technical, and cultural events. Kindly ensure you bring your valid college ID card for verification at the venue.

      • Is accommodation available?

        Accommodation is available at a charge of ₹250 per day, which includes dinner and breakfast.
